## General

### What is BuzzInvitly?

BuzzInvitly is a modern event invitation platform that helps you create beautiful digital invitations, manage guest lists, track RSVPs, and analyze event engagement. Think of it as Canva + Evite combined with powerful analytics.

### Who is BuzzInvitly for?

BuzzInvitly is perfect for:
- **Personal users**: Birthday parties, weddings, baby showers, holidays
- **Event planners**: Professional event management and client events
- **Businesses**: Corporate events, team gatherings, conferences
- **Organizations**: Community events, fundraisers, meetings

### How is BuzzInvitly different from other invitation platforms?

Key differences:
- **Modern design editor** with Fabric.js canvas technology
- **Buzz Score algorithm** to measure event excitement
- **Flexible pricing** with both subscriptions and pay-per-use coins
- **Multi-channel delivery** (email, SMS, shareable links)
- **Advanced analytics** with detailed insights
- **PWA support** for mobile app experience
- **Developer API** for integrations (BUSINESS plan)

### Is BuzzInvitly free?

Yes! Our FREE plan includes:
- 5 events per month
- 50 guests per event
- 30+ basic templates
- Shareable links (free delivery)
- Basic analytics

You only pay if you need more features or want to send email/SMS invitations.

### Do I need to download an app?

No download required! BuzzInvitly works in your web browser. However, you can install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for an app-like experience on mobile devices.

### What browsers are supported?

BuzzInvitly works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome 90+ ✅
- Firefox 88+ ✅
- Safari 14+ ✅
- Edge 90+ ✅

**Mobile browsers**:
- iOS Safari 14+ ✅
- Chrome for Android 90+ ✅

### Can I use my Google account to sign up?

Yes! We support Google OAuth for quick sign-up and login.

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click "Upgrade"

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime:

- **Analytics** data is retained

### Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% off for:

## Events & Invitations

### How many events can I create?

Depends on your plan:
- **FREE**: 5 events per month
- **PRO**: Unlimited
- **BUSINESS**: Unlimited

### How many guests can I invite per event?

Depends on your plan:
- **FREE**: 50 guests per event
- **PRO**: 500 guests per event
- **BUSINESS**: Unlimited

### Can I send test invitations?

Yes! Before sending to all guests:
1. Add yourself as a test guest
2. Send invitation to yourself
3. Review the invitation
4. Make any changes
5. Send to all guests

### How do I send invitations?

Three delivery methods:

**1. Email** (1 coin each)
- Professional HTML email
- RSVP button included
- Calendar attachment

**2. SMS** (2 coins each)
- Text message with link
- Quick RSVP access
- High open rate (98%)

**3. Share Link** (Free)
- Copy and paste anywhere
- QR code available
- Social media friendly

### Can I schedule invitations to send later?

Yes! When sending invitations:
1. Select guests
2. Click "Schedule Send"
3. Choose date and time
4. Confirm

Invitations will be sent automatically at the scheduled time.

### Can I send reminder emails?

Yes! Automatic reminders are sent:
- 7 days before RSVP deadline
- 3 days before RSVP deadline
- 1 day before RSVP deadline

Only sent to guests who haven't responded. No extra cost.

### What if an invitation fails to send?

Failed invitations are highlighted in red. Common reasons:
- Invalid email address
- Invalid phone number
- Email bounced
- SMS delivery failed

You can retry sending to failed guests.

### Can I edit an event after sending invitations?

Yes! You can edit event details anytime. Guests will see updated info when they access their invitation. For major changes, we recommend sending an update email.

## Guest Management

### How do I add guests?

Three methods:

**1. Manual Entry**
- Click "Add Guest"
- Enter name, email, phone
- Set plus-ones

**2. CSV Import**
- Download CSV template
- Fill in guest info
- Upload CSV file

**3. Copy-Paste**
- Paste comma-separated emails
- Bulk add multiple guests

### What information do I need for each guest?

**Required**:
- Name

**Optional**:
- Email (needed for email invitations)
- Phone (needed for SMS invitations)
- Plus-ones (how many guests they can bring)
- Group/category (for organization)

### Can guests bring plus-ones?

Yes! Set plus-ones per guest:
- 0 = No plus-ones allowed
- 1 = Can bring 1 guest
- 2 = Can bring 2 guests
- Unlimited = Can bring any number

Guests can specify names of plus-ones when they RSVP.

### How do I import guests from a CSV file?

1. Click "Import Guests"
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in columns: name, email, phone, plusOnes
4. Save as CSV
5. Upload file
6. Review and confirm

**Example CSV**:
```csv
name,email,phone,plusOnes
John Doe,john@example.com,+1234567890,2
Jane Smith,jane@example.com,+1234567891,0
```

### Can I organize guests into groups?

Yes! Tag guests with categories:
- Family
- Friends
- Coworkers
- Neighbors
- VIP

Filter and send invitations by group.

### Can I export my guest list?

Yes! Export formats:
- CSV (all data)
- Excel (formatted)
- PDF (printable)

Export options:
- All guests
- Attending only
- By RSVP status

### What if I need to remove a guest?

Click the trash icon next to their name. This:
- Removes them from guest list
- Invalidates their RSVP link
- Does not send notification

### Can guests update their own information?

Yes! When they RSVP, guests can:
- Update their name
- Add plus-one names
- Add dietary restrictions
- Add notes/comments

## RSVP Tracking

### How do guests RSVP?

Guests receive an invitation with:
- **Email**: RSVP button in email
- **SMS**: Link to RSVP page
- **Share Link**: Direct RSVP page

They click and select:
- Attending
- Not Attending
- Maybe

### Can guests change their RSVP?

Yes! Guests can update their RSVP anytime before the deadline. You'll be notified of changes.

### Do I get notified when someone RSVPs?

Yes! You receive:
- Email notification (can be disabled)
- Push notification (if PWA installed)
- Real-time dashboard update

### What is an RSVP deadline?

The last date/time guests can respond. After the deadline:
- RSVP link still works (configurable)
- Shows as "late" response
- Helps you finalize headcount

### Can I set different RSVP deadlines?

One deadline per event. However, you can:
- Extend the deadline
- Send personal reminders
- Accept late RSVPs manually

### What happens if someone doesn't RSVP?

Non-responders:
- Receive automatic reminders
- Show as "Pending" in your dashboard
- You can follow up personally
- Can still RSVP after deadline (your choice)

### Can I manually mark someone as attending?

Yes! Click on guest → Edit → Change RSVP status. Useful for:
- Phone RSVPs
- Late responses
- Special circumstances
