## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [buzzinvitly.com](https://buzzinvitly.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Recent Events**: Your latest events
- **Quick Actions**: Create event, buy coins, view analytics
- **Statistics**: Events created, guests invited, RSVP rate
- **Coin Balance**: Your available coins

## Creating Your First Event

### Step 1: Choose a Template

1. Click **Create Event** from the dashboard
2. Browse templates by category:
   - Birthday Parties
   - Weddings
   - Corporate Events
   - Baby Showers
   - Holidays
   - Casual Gatherings

3. Preview templates by hovering over them
4. Click **Use This Template** on your favorite

**Template Types**:
- **Free Templates**: Available to all users
- **Premium Templates**: Require PRO subscription or 10 coins

### Step 2: Enter Event Details

Fill in your event information:

- **Event Name**: "Sarah's 30th Birthday Bash"
- **Event Type**: Birthday, Wedding, Corporate, etc.
- **Date & Time**: When your event takes place
- **Location**: Venue name and address
- **Description**: Details about your event
- **RSVP Deadline**: When guests must respond by

**Pro Tip**: Add as much detail as possible to reduce guest questions!

### Step 3: Customize Your Invitation

Click **Customize Design** to open the design editor.

## Using the Design Editor

The design editor is a powerful canvas-based tool for customizing your invitation.

### Interface Overview

- **Canvas**: Center area showing your invitation
- **Toolbar**: Top controls for zoom, undo, redo
- **Object Panel**: Left sidebar with text, images, shapes
- **Property Panel**: Right sidebar to edit selected objects

### Adding Text

1. Click **Add Text** button
2. Click on the canvas where you want text
3. Type your text
4. Customize in the property panel:
   - **Font**: Choose from 15+ Google Fonts
   - **Size**: Adjust text size
   - **Color**: Pick any color
   - **Alignment**: Left, center, right
   - **Style**: Bold, italic, underline

**Available Fonts**:
- Playfair Display (elegant)
- Montserrat (modern)
- Roboto (clean)
- Dancing Script (handwritten)
- Lora (traditional)
- And 10 more!

### Adding Images

1. Click **Add Image** button
2. Upload from your computer (PNG, JPG, up to 5MB)
3. Image appears on canvas
4. Resize by dragging corners
5. Move by dragging the image

**Image Tips**:
- Use high-resolution images (300 DPI)
- PNG files support transparency
- Keep file sizes under 2MB for best performance

### Working with Objects

**Select an object**: Click on it
**Move**: Drag with mouse
**Resize**: Drag corner handles
**Rotate**: Use rotation handle
**Delete**: Select and press Delete key

**Layering**:
- **Bring to Front**: Moves object above all others
- **Send to Back**: Moves object behind all others

### Zoom & Navigation

- **Zoom In**: Click + button or Ctrl/Cmd + +
- **Zoom Out**: Click - button or Ctrl/Cmd + -
- **Fit to Screen**: Click fit button
- **Pan**: Hold spacebar and drag

### Saving Your Design

Your design auto-saves every 30 seconds. You can also:
- Click **Save** button to save manually
- Click **Preview** to see full-size preview
- Click **Download** to save as image (PNG, JPG)

## Managing Guests

### Adding Guests Manually

1. Go to your event page
2. Click **Guest List** tab
3. Click **Add Guest**
4. Enter guest information:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (for email invitations)
   - Phone (for SMS invitations)
   - Plus-ones (how many guests they can bring)

### Importing Guests (CSV)

1. Click **Import Guests**
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in guest information
4. Upload the completed CSV
5. Review and confirm imports

**CSV Format**:
```csv
name,email,phone,plusOnes
John Doe,john@example.com,+1234567890,2
Jane Smith,jane@example.com,+1234567891,0
```

### Managing Guest List

- **Edit Guest**: Click pencil icon
- **Delete Guest**: Click trash icon
- **Filter**: By RSVP status (All, Attending, Not Attending, Maybe, Pending)
- **Search**: Find guests by name or email
- **Export**: Download guest list as CSV

### Plus-Ones

Allow guests to bring additional people:
- Set plus-ones per guest (0, 1, 2, unlimited)
- Guests can specify names of plus-ones
- Track total headcount including plus-ones

## Sending Invitations

### Delivery Methods

BuzzInvitly supports three delivery methods:

#### 1. Email Invitations
- **Cost**: 1 coin per email
- **Best for**: Professional events, formal invitations
- **Features**:
  - Beautiful HTML emails
  - RSVP button in email
  - Automatic reminders
  - Calendar attachments (.ics)

#### 2. SMS Invitations
- **Cost**: 2 coins per SMS
- **Best for**: Last-minute invites, casual events
- **Features**:
  - Text message with invitation link
  - Quick RSVP via link
  - High open rates (98%)

#### 3. Share Link
- **Cost**: Free
- **Best for**: Social media, messaging apps
- **Features**:
  - Unique URL for your event
  - Share via WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram
  - QR code for easy scanning

### Sending Process

1. **Select Recipients**: Choose which guests to invite
2. **Choose Method**: Email, SMS, or both
3. **Customize Message**: Add personal message (optional)
4. **Preview**: Review invitation before sending
5. **Send**: Click **Send Invitations**

**Confirmation**:
- You'll see a success message with number of invitations sent
- Failed sends will be highlighted for retry

### Invitation Tracking

Track invitation delivery status:
- **Sent**: Invitation delivered successfully
- **Opened**: Recipient opened the invitation
- **Clicked**: Recipient clicked RSVP button
- **Failed**: Delivery failed (invalid email/phone)

### Buzz Score Algorithm

The **Buzz Score** measures how excited people are about your event (0-100):

**Factors**:
- RSVP Response Rate (30%)
- Attendance Rate (30%)
- Speed of Responses (20%)
- Invitation Engagement (10%)
- Social Shares (10%)

**Score Ranges**:
- **90-100**: Viral Event - Incredible buzz!
- **70-89**: Hot Event - High excitement
- **50-69**: Good Event - Solid interest
- **30-49**: Moderate Event - Decent response
- **0-29**: Quiet Event - Low engagement

**How to Improve Your Buzz Score**:
1. Send invitations early (4-6 weeks before)
2. Use eye-catching designs
3. Follow up with non-responders
4. Create shareable content
5. Engage with guests via updates

## Mobile App (PWA)

### Installing the App

BuzzInvitly is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works like a native mobile app.

**On iOS (Safari)**:
1. Visit buzzinvitly.com
2. Tap Share button
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android (Chrome)**:
1. Visit buzzinvitly.com
2. Tap menu (3 dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

### Mobile Features

- **Offline Access**: View events without internet
- **Push Notifications**: Real-time RSVP alerts
- **Quick Actions**: Create event from home screen
- **Fast & Responsive**: Optimized for mobile
- **App-like Experience**: Fullscreen, no browser UI

### Mobile Navigation

- **Bottom Nav Bar**: Quick access to Dashboard, Templates, Events, Profile
- **Hamburger Menu**: Full navigation sidebar
- **Touch Optimized**: Large tap targets, swipe gestures

### Offline Mode

When offline, you can:
- View previously loaded events

**Cannot do offline**:

## Tips & Best Practices

### Creating Invitations

**Do**:
- Send invitations 4-6 weeks before the event
- Use high-quality images (300 DPI)
- Include all essential details (date, time, location)
- Set a clear RSVP deadline (1-2 weeks before event)
- Proofread everything before sending

**Don't**:
- Wait until the last minute
- Use low-resolution or blurry images
- Overcrowd the design with too much text
- Forget to include RSVP deadline
- Send without previewing first

### Managing Guests

**Do**:
- Import guest lists via CSV for bulk uploads
- Double-check email addresses for accuracy
- Segment guests by group (family, friends, colleagues)
- Allow appropriate plus-ones
- Export guest list as backup

**Don't**:
- Add duplicate guests
- Use generic email addresses (info@, contact@)
- Forget to update guest info when changed
- Send to unverified email addresses

### Boosting Attendance

**Do**:
- Create visually appealing invitations
- Send reminder emails (automated)
- Follow up personally with VIP guests
- Make RSVP process easy (one click)
- Share event on social media

**Don't**:
- Spam guests with too many reminders
- Change event details after sending invitations
- Make RSVP process complicated
- Ignore guest questions or concerns

### Design Best Practices

**Do**:
- Keep text readable (minimum 12pt font)
- Use contrasting colors for text/background
- Align elements for professional look
- Use high-quality fonts
- Leave white space (don't overcrowd)

**Don't**:
- Use more than 3 different fonts
- Place text over busy backgrounds
- Make text too small or too large
- Use clashing colors
- Forget to spell-check

### Maximizing Buzz Score

**Do**:
- Send invitations early
- Use engaging designs
- Follow up with non-responders
- Encourage social shares
- Keep guests excited with updates

**Don't**:
- Send invitations too late
- Use boring or generic templates
- Ignore non-responders
- Forget to remind guests
- Go silent until event day

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I edit an invitation after sending?
Yes! You can edit event details anytime. Guests will see updated info when they click the RSVP link. For major changes, consider sending an update email.

### What happens if a guest changes their RSVP?
Guests can update their RSVP anytime before the deadline. You'll be notified of changes, and analytics update automatically.

### Can I get a refund on unused coins?
Coins are non-refundable but never expire. You can use them anytime for any event.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Can I transfer my account to someone else?
Yes, contact support@buzzinvitly.com to transfer account ownership.

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS), never share your data, and comply with GDPR/CCPA. See [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md).

### Can I use BuzzInvitly for commercial events?
Absolutely! Our BUSINESS plan is perfect for professional event planners and corporate events.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes! Contact us at support@buzzinvitly.com for nonprofit pricing.
